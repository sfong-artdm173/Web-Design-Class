Who Am I?

With over 9 years of experience in the marketing field, I've become well-versed in digital and operational marketing for B2B and B2C companies. Let's work together to build an image, create awareness, surprise, inform and/or generate sales for your brand.

What I Can Do

Marketing Strategy

Find what resonates with your target audience and builds a game plan for how to attract new clients or repeat customers to your business.

Email Marketing

I have your back on improving your email readability and performance. I specialize in developing and executing effective email marketing and nuture email campaigns.

Graphic Design

I've been creating print and digital graphic design assets since the beginning of my career. You name it, I've know the specs on how to do it.

Web Design

Stand out from your competition with a professional website. I can code your website from scratch or, if you want more editing power, I can develop your site on a CMS.

Website Management

Boosting your online presence is what I like to do. From improving your SEO to regular maintainance to creating landing pages for easy conversions.

Performance Marketing

Whether it's through social media marketing, paid search, or more. I'll make sure we are optimizing each campaign to get the biggest bang for your buck.

Website Analytics

Having trouble parcing through all the data from your website? Let me help analyze and compile your data into easy to read reports.

Lead Management

Cleaning, segmenting and rating incoming leads are my bread and butter. I relish in optimizing lead flow throughout the pipeline.

SMS Marketing

Want to start texting your subscribers or customers but don't know where to start? Allow my expertise to assist you on navigating through the set up and management.
